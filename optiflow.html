01 — The brief

Why go Premium.

Optiflow had earned the brand-registry status to unlock Premium A+ — Amazon's top content tier — but a bigger canvas only helps if the design actually uses it. Standard modules dropped into a premium slot just look like standard modules with more whitespace.

The brief was to build a true Premium experience: full-width imagery, interactive hotspots, a hero video and a navigable comparison table — a detail page that feels less like a listing and more like a flagship microsite living inside Amazon.

02 — The idea

The canvas is the message.

Premium A+ removes the gutters, so I designed edge-to-edge — letting imagery run full-bleed and using scale itself to signal that Optiflow is a premium brand before a word is read.

Every premium-only module earns its place: hotspots reveal detail on demand, the video carries the product in motion, and the comparison table lets shoppers self-select — so the page does the selling interactively instead of asking anyone to read a wall of text.

Full-bleed brand hero

Premium A+ unlocks an edge-to-edge banner, so the page opens on a cinematic, full-width statement of what Optiflow is and who it's for — scale doing the premium positioning before any copy.

2

Interactive hotspots

A premium-only module where shoppers hover or tap points on the product to reveal feature callouts. It turns a static spec list into exploration — answering "what does this do?" the moment it's asked.

3

Hero video

An auto-playing video module — exclusive to Premium A+ — shows the product in motion and in context. Seeing it work removes the doubt that still images leave behind.

4

Image carousel

A swipeable gallery packs lifestyle shots, detail crops and use-cases into a single slot — depth without forcing an endless scroll, and a layout that feels native on mobile.

5

Comparison table

An enhanced cross-sell table maps the range side by side so a shopper can self-select the right Optiflow for them — lifting basket size while genuinely helping them decide.

6

FAQ & brand close

An accordion FAQ pre-empts the last objections, then a closing brand panel loops the story back to the promise in the hero — so the experience resolves instead of just ending.

04 — The outcome

A flagship that lives on Amazon.

The finished Premium A+ story gives Optiflow a detail page with the polish of a standalone brand site — interactive, full-canvas and built to convert the shoppers who research before they buy.
